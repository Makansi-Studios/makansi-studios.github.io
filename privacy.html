Overview

ACT Ascent is developed and operated by Makansi Studios. This privacy policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a user.

Information We Collect

ACT Ascent stores your quiz progress, streak data, firewood levels, and app preferences locally on your device. This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any external server.

Payments

If you subscribe to Summit Pro, payment is processed entirely through Apple's App Store. Makansi Studios does not collect, store, or have access to your payment information at any point.

Information We Do Not Collect

Makansi Studios does not collect your name, email address, location, or any personally identifiable information. ACT Ascent does not require you to create an account.

Children's Privacy

ACT Ascent is intended for high school students aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Third Party Services

ACT Ascent uses RevenueCat to manage subscription status. RevenueCat's privacy policy can be found at revenuecat.com/privacy.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

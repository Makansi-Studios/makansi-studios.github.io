Frequently Asked Questions

How do I unlock unlimited quizzes?

Upgrade to Summit Pro from within the app. Summit Pro includes unlimited quizzes, unlimited daily challenges, and full access to all four mountains. Try it free for 7 days.

How do I restore my purchase?

Open ACT Ascent, navigate to the paywall screen, and tap "Restore Purchases" at the bottom of the screen.

My progress isn't saving — what do I do?

Your progress is saved locally on your device. Make sure you have sufficient storage available. If the issue persists, try restarting the app.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your Summit Pro subscription at any time through your iPhone's Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions.
